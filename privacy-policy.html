Information We Collect

We collect the minimum information necessary to provide the app's features. Here's exactly what that means:

📊
Session Data

Scores, duration, word counts, and speech metrics from your practice sessions. Stored only on your device.

🔑
Account Info

If you sign in with Google: your name, email address, and profile photo — used to link your subscription.

💳
Purchase Records

Subscription status and transaction IDs from Google Play or Apple App Store. We never see your payment details.

📱
Device Info

OS version, device model, and a random ad identifier — only if you are on the free tier with ads enabled.

02 —

Microphone & Audio Processing

SpeakSmart requires microphone access (RECORD_AUDIO) solely to transcribe your speech during practice sessions. This permission is the core function of the app.

Permission Why It's Needed Status
RECORD_AUDIO Microphone access Live speech transcription during practice sessions using on-device speech-to-text REQUIRED
INTERNET Network access Subscription verification, authentication, and serving ads to free users REQUIRED
BILLING In-app purchases Processing Pro subscriptions and one-time purchases via Google Play REQUIRED
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED Device startup Restoring daily reminder notifications after device reboot OPTIONAL

🎙 Audio is never recorded as a file, uploaded, or transmitted. The microphone stream is passed directly to Android's built-in SpeechRecognizer API (on-device). Raw audio is discarded immediately after transcription. Only the resulting text transcript is stored, locally on your device.

03 —

How We Use Information

Your data is used for the following purposes — nothing else:

  • Analysing your speech patterns to generate coaching scores and feedback
  • Storing session history so you can track your progress over time
  • Verifying and restoring your Pro subscription across devices and reinstalls
  • Displaying relevant advertisements to free users via Google AdMob
  • Sending optional daily practice reminder notifications (if enabled)
  • Improving app stability by diagnosing crashes (anonymised, via Firebase Crashlytics)

We do not use your data to build advertising profiles, sell to third parties, or train AI/ML models.

04 —

Data Storage & Security

All session data — transcripts, scores, metrics, and history — is stored in a local SQLite database on your device. This data is not backed up to any external server by default.

If you sign in with Google, your account information is stored in Firebase Authentication. Firebase is a Google service governed by Google's privacy policy and is operated on infrastructure that complies with ISO 27001, SOC 1, SOC 2, and SOC 3 standards.

🗑 Deleting your account from within the app (Settings → Account → Delete Account) permanently removes all locally stored data and delinks your Google account. Subscription records held by Google Play or Apple are governed by their respective policies.

06 —

Advertising

Free users see advertisements served by Google AdMob. These ads may be personalised based on your device's advertising ID and general usage patterns as permitted by your device-level ad personalisation settings.

  • Pro subscribers see no advertisements of any kind
  • You can opt out of personalised ads in your device settings (Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation)
  • We do not pass any voice data, transcripts, or health information to advertisers
07 —

Children's Privacy

SpeakSmart is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

08 —

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data from within the app at any time
  • Correction: Update your account information via Google Account settings
  • Portability: Export your session history from within the app (Pro feature)
  • Opt-out of ads: Upgrade to Pro or adjust your device-level ad settings
  • Microphone revocation: Revoke microphone permission in device settings at any time (this will disable speech recording functionality)

Residents of California (CCPA), the European Economic Area (GDPR), and other jurisdictions with applicable privacy laws may submit data requests via the contact details below. We will respond within 30 days.
